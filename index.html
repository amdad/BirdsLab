The Services I offer

Digital Art

Sometimes what you need is a great painting of chicago vectorized to scale to every size and appropriate for all realms of digital media. Logos, graphics, twitter pages, slide shows, I do them all.

User Experience

The experience your users have using your website, is as important as its look and feel. Users are picky and your end user is no different. Different end users have different expectations and a good product must meet and exceed every one.

Web Design

Designing a good website starts before you have a single line of code down. You have to plan out every step along the way to ensure that your end product's look and feel is appropriate and that you catch your users with their very first glance.

Product Management

Taking an idea and turning it into a profitable product is no easy task, especially on the internet. You have to fight through hundreds or even thousands of competitors. You need to market effectively and above all turn a profit. I can help.

Appgraft Logo

Its a tree!

A commission, I decided early on that I wanted to do something unique. The first step I usually take when working on a logo is to troll through google images looking for inspiration. The main thing I found was that 95% of tree logos look identical. I worked closely with the client on this one, usually sending him a few versions a day until we got it where we were both happy with it. He uses it as his screensaver now, highest praise there is.

Community Voice International

A wonderful charity!

This was a givecamp charity project I worked on. The client and I sat down right off the bat and hammered out a list of exactly what he wanted, and how we were going to do it. Before the charity he had requested a 'UX' guy and there were only two designers working the entire charity. It was an exhausting and rewarding weekend to say the least.

Fluent Finch Logo

Birds!

A particularly challenging commission. I didn't want to do 'just another bird logo' Twitter has ruined birds for graphic designers. Researching finches I noticed that they had very specific looks, and decided to use their own natural colour palette to add a degree of realism. (Although I would like to state, for the record, Finches are stupid looking birds.)

'TCS' Mockup Design

Mockups shall set you free./p>

This is a mock up design of a project I worked on. What is always step 1 for me when working on any sort of website or application is to always sit down and knock out a mockup. Ideally with the client present, so they can add direct feedback. From here I start adding in graphical elements in photoshop to nail down the desired look and feel of the final product.

'Admiral Vic' Logo

Chicago Skyline

The Chicago Skyline looking north from the Shedd Aquarium. I took a vacation there a few years ago and the view was so striking, I had to paint it. If you're ever at the Shedd, look north across the water from the patio near the food court. Its as beautiful in real life.
